Olist Sales & Customer Analytics



Purpose of This Project

This project was designed to replicate a real-world business analytics environment where raw transactional data is transformed into meaningful insights that support strategic decision-making.

It demonstrates the full capability of turning data into actionable business intelligence.



Analytical Impact

The project successfully delivered:

* A unified analytical dataset from multiple sources
* Clear visibility into revenue drivers
* Customer behavior insights
* Product performance rankings
* Operational efficiency evaluation
* Geographic business analysis



Business Impact

The analysis enables businesses to:

* Identify high-value customers and segments
* Improve customer retention strategies
* Optimize product category performance
* Reduce operational inefficiencies
* Improve delivery performance
* Expand into high-growth regions



Technical Impact

This project demonstrates hands-on capability in:

* Data integration across multiple datasets
* SQL-based data extraction and transformation
* Python-based exploratory data analysis
* KPI development and tracking
* Dashboard design and storytelling



Professional Value
